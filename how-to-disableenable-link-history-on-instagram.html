    How To Disable/Enable Link History On Instagram

    Andre Yulianto January 27, 2024

    The Techschumz article has outlined steps to turn on or off the link history, as sometimes you may not want to keep track of opened links.

    Instagram is a social media app that allows users to share photos and videos with their followers. Among all the useful features, there is a link history feature that keeps track of the links that users have clicked on within the app. Sometimes you open a link, but you can scroll to the end and miss the link. This feature can be useful, as it keeps track of articles or websites you want to revisit later.

    However, to have control over your browsing activity and privacy settings on Instagram, you can choose to disable or enable this feature. There are a few simple steps you can follow.

    Turn On Link History on Instagram

    This is how you can enable the link history on our Instagram account.

    Step 1. Navigate to the “Instagram” app and to “your profile page.”

    Step 2. Now tap on the “Menu icon” in the top right corner of the screen.

    Step 3. Choose the “Your Activity” option.

    Step 4. Scroll down the menu and select “Link History.”

    Step 5. Finally, tap on “Allow Link History.”

    Turn off Link History on Instagram

    To turn off the link history on Instagram, follow the steps below.

    Step 1. Go to your “Profile” page.

    Step 2. Then tap on “Your Activity.”

    Step 3. Next, navigate to “Link History.”

    Step 4. Click on “Opened Link.”

    Step 5. Then tap on “Browse Settings.”

    Step 6. Finally, turn off the “Allow Link History.”

    You have disabled the link history on Instagram, meaning the links you open will not be saved to your Instagram account.

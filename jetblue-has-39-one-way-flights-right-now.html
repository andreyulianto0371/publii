    JetBlue Has $39 One-Way Flights Right Now

    Andre Yulianto January 12, 2024

    If you’ve procrastinated on planning your fall travel, wait no longer. JetBlue has released fall deals for one-way flights starting at $39 across their entire domestic network, plus some attractive deals for international destinations. And if none of those locales entice you, Breeze Airways is also offering deals on one-way flights for the fall.

    You can’t sleep on these: JetBlue’s fall travel deals only last for the three days—you need to book your flights no later than Thursday, Aug. 24 at 11:5p p.m. ET. Here’s what you need to know.

    Fly JetBlue this fall for $39

    When you go to the fall sale website, you will automatically see the available fares from your local airport. The number of eligible fall deal flights you’re shown will vary depending on what’s leaving from your local airport. If you’re willing to travel to another nearby airport before taking flight, click on the pencil icon and select “View All Deals” to see additional fares.

    People from Boston, Miami, Orlando, Los Angeles, New York, and San Juan are in the best position, with many deals to choose from, including some cheap international flights.

    While the flights are advertised as one-way, you can also shop deals on return flights. The one-way deals are limited to flights leaving Monday through Thursday and on Saturday, from Sept. 6 through Nov. 15.

    Here are some highlights from the deals available as of the time of this writing:

    • Buffalo (BUF) to Orlando (MCO) $39

    • Charleston (CHS) to New York (JFK) $39

    • Charleston (CHS) to Westchester (HPN) $39

    • Las Vegas (LAS) to San Francisco (SFO) $39

    • Florida (FLL) to New Orleans (MSY) $39

    • San Diego (SAN) to Florida (FLL) $79

    • New York (JFK) to Puerto Rico (BQN) $99

    • New York (JFK) to Bermuda (BDA) $84 

    • Boston (BOS) to Santo Domingo (SDQ) $139

    Another option to consider: JetBlue is offering vacation packages starting at $313 for round-trip flights plus two nights at a hotel; these packages will be displayed to the right of the one-way flight prices.

    • Shut everything out with this travel pillow with an eye mask and earplugs: Neck pillow for travel $23.99

    Some things to keep in mind

    The flights are for JetBlue’s lowest fare classes (Blue Basic and Blue). Blue Basic only gives you one personal item, which must fit under the seat in front of you. Carry-ons will be checked for $65 at the gate. Neither Blue Basic nor Blue includes checked luggage, which costs $35 for the first checked bag and $45 for the second. You might see additional restrictions on the days that you can fly, limited travel windows, and blackout dates, depending on your route. Read the general terms and restrictions here.

    Breeze Airways is offering 30% off for fall flights

    If JetBlue’s options don’t entice you, Breeze Airways is also offering fall flight deals. The airline is discounting prices to over 50 nonstop routes, with fares that start at $39.

    This deal will likewise only last for a few days. Use the code “YOUDOYOU” at checkout after choosing your flight on their website. Book your departure by 11:59 p.m. ET on Aug. 24 for flights leaving between Sept. 5–Dec. 19. (There are blackout dates on Nov. 16–28.) The 30% off applies only to the base fare price, so expect to pay the full taxes and fare fees. The fares are for their most basic “Nice” tier, which, like JetBlue’s Blue Basic, only includes a personal item (it’s $37 for a carry-on bag). Read the terms and conditions of the deal here.

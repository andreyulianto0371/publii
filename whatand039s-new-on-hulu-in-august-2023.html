    What&#039;s New on Hulu in August 2023

    Andre Yulianto January 12, 2024

    The streaming era has changed a lot about how TV works—so much so that both the Writers Guild and the Screen Actors Guild are on strike together for the first time since Eisenhower was president and Ronald Reagan was considered a labor leader. But one thing will likely never change: Good shows ending way too soon, usually because the corporate fat cats decided to cancel them. On that note, on Aug. 2, Hulu will begin the farewell tour for one of its best ongoing series, Reservation Dogs.

    Launched amid the pandemic, the comedy about a group of Indigenous teen misfits living on a reservation in Oklahoma gained significant critical acclaim and a cult audience, but never broke out in a big way quite like the streamer’s other recent hit, Only Murders in the Building (also returning for season three on Aug. 8). Despite the cancelation, if you haven’t watched it yet, it’s time for a binge: It’s a perfect blend of unique, funny, and heartfelt, with incisive writing and truly great characters.

    Here’s everything else coming to Hulu in August, including the final season of Breeders (Aug. 1) and the ultra-budge 2022 horror flick Skinamarink (Aug. 4).

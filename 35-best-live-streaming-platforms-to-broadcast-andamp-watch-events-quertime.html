    35 Best Live Streaming Platforms to Broadcast &amp; Watch Events - Quertime

    Andre Yulianto January 16, 2024

    Undeniably live streaming is one of the most popular online activities that occupy a significant amount of Internet users’ time. Be it a sports events, music concerts, TV shows or world news, you can watch them live online, either free or paid subscription.

    Due to the advancement in high speed Internet and popularity of mobile devices, big companies like Apple and Google have entered the online TV markets. It shows how important live video streaming is these days. So if you are an Internet marker, e-commerce entrepreneur or content publisher, live streaming is something you absolutely can’t afford to miss!

    Today we’ve rounded up 30 most popular live streaming services where you can live stream any shows and events by connecting your webcam, camcorder, or mobile device to the Internet. These platforms offer all the necessary video and broadcast tools so that you can reach your target audience easily. Apart from that, social sharing features are also available where you can embed video on your blog, or share it on Facebook, Twitter X and other social media.

    1. YouTube Live Streaming

    YouTube Live Streaming is a versatile platform enabling users to broadcast and watch events seamlessly. Boasting a user-friendly interface, it caters to a global audience, supporting various content types, from gaming to educational sessions. With real-time interaction features like live chat, viewers can engage with content creators, fostering a sense of community. The platform also facilitates easy monetization, making it appealing for professionals and amateurs alike. Its integration with other Google services enhances accessibility and content management.

    2. Facebook Live

    Facebook Live provides a dynamic and social streaming experience, allowing users to share live events with their Facebook audience effortlessly. With a vast user base, it offers extensive reach and engagement. The platform supports diverse content, including personal broadcasts, Q&A sessions, and behind-the-scenes glimpses. Its interactive features, such as real-time reactions and comments, enhance viewer participation. Integration with other Facebook tools amplifies promotional efforts, making it an effective choice for individuals and businesses aiming to connect with their audience in real-time.

    3. Instagram Live Broadcast

    Instagram Live Broadcast offers an intimate and ephemeral streaming experience within the popular photo-sharing platform. Suited for spontaneous and informal content, it allows users to connect with their followers instantly. The ephemeral nature of the broadcasts encourages a sense of urgency, attracting viewers to join in real-time. With features like live comments and emoji reactions, engagement is immediate and interactive. Ideal for influencers, brands, and content creators, Instagram Live Broadcast enables authentic, unfiltered connections, creating a unique and personal touch to online events.

    4. Google Meet Live Stream

    Google Meet Live Stream provides a seamless platform for broadcasting and watching events. Designed as an extension of the popular Google Meet video conferencing tool, it offers a straightforward interface for hosting live streams within the Google ecosystem. Users can easily initiate a live stream directly from a Google Meet call, making it an ideal choice for educational webinars, corporate meetings, or virtual events. With robust security features and integration with other Google Workspace apps, it ensures a secure and collaborative live streaming experience.

    5. X Twitter Live Stream

    X Twitter Live Stream, powered by Twitter’s cutting-edge technology, allows users to share live video content with a global audience. With a user-friendly interface, it enables effortless live streaming directly from the Twitter app. The platform supports real-time interactions through comments and likes, fostering engagement and connection among viewers. Whether for personal broadcasting or professional events, X Live makes it easy to reach and captivate a diverse audience on the widely used social media platform.

    6. TikTok Live

    TikTok Live introduces an immersive live streaming experience to its vibrant community. With its short-form video focus, TikTok Live allows users to connect with followers in real time, share spontaneous moments, and showcase talents. The platform encourages viewer interaction through comments, virtual gifts, and collaborations, enhancing the sense of community during live broadcasts. TikTok Live is ideal for individuals and content creators looking to engage with a younger demographic through creative and entertaining live content.

    7. LinkedIn Live

    LinkedIn Live elevates professional networking by offering a platform for live streaming events and discussions. Geared towards business professionals, it provides a polished environment for hosting webinars, conferences, and product launches. LinkedIn Live integrates seamlessly with the LinkedIn platform, enabling broadcasters to reach a targeted audience within the professional community. With features such as Q&A sessions and audience engagement metrics, it facilitates meaningful connections and knowledge sharing in a corporate context.

    8. Netflix Live Events

    While Netflix primarily focuses on on-demand content, it occasionally hosts live events for special occasions or premieres. These events provide a communal viewing experience, allowing subscribers to simultaneously watch and discuss the content in real-time. Netflix Live Events are often interactive, featuring live chats and Q&A sessions with cast and crew. Though not a constant feature, these live events enhance the overall user experience and engagement, turning the typically solitary streaming platform into a communal space during specific, highly anticipated moments.

    9. Twitch TV

    Twitch TV is a powerhouse in live streaming, especially for gaming enthusiasts. With a vibrant community and dedicated channels for various interests, Twitch offers a personalized and interactive experience. Gamers can broadcast their gameplay, engage with viewers through chat, and build a loyal following. Beyond gaming, Twitch hosts diverse content, including music, art, and talk shows. The platform’s unique features, such as Bits and subscriptions, enable content creators to monetize their channels effectively. Twitch’s emphasis on community-building and live interaction has solidified its status as a go-to platform for those seeking real-time engagement and entertainment.

    10. Zoom

    Zoom is a versatile live streaming platform recognized for its video conferencing capabilities, making it a powerful tool for hosting and attending virtual events. Whether used for webinars, meetings, or live broadcasts, Zoom provides a seamless user experience. With its intuitive interface, high-quality video, and interactive features like Q&A sessions and chat, Zoom has become a go-to platform for various live events.

    11. Dailymotion Live Streaming

    Dailymotion Live Streaming offers a professional-grade solution for broadcasting events to a global audience. Leveraging Dailymotion’s extensive reach, this platform ensures high-quality streaming and reliable performance. With customizable features for branding and monetization, Dailymotion is suitable for both personal and business use. Its robust analytics tools enable content creators to assess audience engagement and optimize their live streaming strategies effectively.

    12. Vimeo Livestream

    Vimeo Livestream delivers high-quality live streaming for a diverse range of events. Known for its reliability and customization options, Vimeo Livestream allows users to tailor the viewing experience with branding and interactive elements. The platform supports multi-camera setups, making it suitable for professional broadcasts, conferences, and live performances. Additionally, Vimeo Livestream provides robust analytics tools, enabling content creators to track viewer engagement and make data-driven decisions. With its emphasis on quality and versatility, Vimeo Livestream is a go-to choice for those seeking a comprehensive live streaming solution for various types of events.

    13. IBM – Video Streaming Platform

    IBM’s Video Streaming Platform stands out for its enterprise-grade capabilities. Tailored for businesses, it provides a secure and scalable solution for delivering live and on-demand video content. With advanced features like content monetization, analytics, and reliable delivery, IBM’s platform empowers organizations to engage audiences and achieve their streaming objectives with confidence.

    14. Restream

    Restream is a versatile live streaming platform that stands out for its ability to simultaneously broadcast content across multiple platforms, maximizing reach. With support for various social media networks and streaming services, Restream simplifies the process of reaching diverse audiences. The platform provides robust analytics and chat features, enhancing engagement and monitoring performance.

    15. Panopto Live Streaming

    Panopto Live Streaming offers a comprehensive solution for educational and corporate live events. With advanced video content management and analytics, Panopto facilitates secure and interactive streaming. Its integration with learning management systems ensures seamless educational experiences, while features like multi-camera support and video editing enhance the production quality.

    16. On24 Go Live

    On24 Go Live is a cutting-edge live streaming platform designed for seamless event broadcasting and viewing. With an intuitive interface, it offers robust features such as interactive engagement tools, analytics, and customization options. On24 Go Live empowers businesses to elevate virtual events, webinars, and conferences, ensuring an immersive and dynamic experience for both presenters and audiences. Its comprehensive analytics provide valuable insights, enhancing post-event assessments.

    17. Vaughn Live

    Vaughn Live caters to a community-driven audience, offering a platform for individuals to broadcast and watch live content. Known for its simplicity and social integration, Vaughn Live encourages user interaction through chat and comments. It’s an excellent choice for those seeking a more grassroots, community-oriented live streaming experience.

    18. JW Player Live Streaming

    JW Player Live Streaming is a comprehensive solution for both live and on-demand content delivery. Renowned for its adaptive streaming technology, JW Player ensures a seamless viewing experience across devices. With features like real-time analytics, monetization options, and easy integration, it caters to businesses and content creators looking to deliver professional live streaming experiences.

    19. Livestorm

    Livestorm is a user-friendly live streaming platform designed for webinars and virtual events. Its browser-based approach eliminates the need for downloads, ensuring accessibility for participants. Livestorm excels in engagement tools, offering interactive features like polls, Q&A, and participant feedback to create dynamic and immersive experiences.

    20. Kaltura Streaming Platform

    Kaltura Streaming Platform is a robust solution catering to large-scale enterprises and educational institutions. With a focus on secure and scalable video delivery, Kaltura provides end-to-end live streaming capabilities, including content creation, distribution, and monetization. Its adaptive streaming technology ensures a seamless viewing experience across devices.

    21. Tinychat

    Tinychat is an intuitive live streaming platform that effortlessly combines video conferencing and social interaction. With its user-friendly interface, Tinychat enables users to easily create and join chat rooms, fostering real-time engagement. It’s particularly popular for spontaneous, casual conversations and live events. The platform supports webcam streaming, screen sharing, and text chat, making it versatile for various purposes, from gaming to professional discussions.

    22. YouNow – Live Stream Video Chat

    YouNow stands out as a live streaming platform designed for the social media era. Catering to a primarily youthful audience, it offers a dynamic space for content creators to showcase talents and connect with fans in real-time. The platform encourages audience participation through live chat and virtual gifts. With its focus on individual expression and authenticity, YouNow has become a hub for emerging talent across diverse genres, including music, gaming, and lifestyle content.

    Brightcove Live is a robust live streaming solution tailored for businesses and enterprises. Brightcove Live supports adaptive bitrate streaming, monetization options, and analytics, ensuring a seamless and monetizable streaming experience. This platform is ideal for organizations seeking top-notch video quality, security, and customization for their live events.

    24. Cloudcast

    Cloudcast redefines live streaming by offering a feature-rich platform that excels in both functionality and user experience. It is a go-to solution for webinars, virtual events, and online conferences. Cloudcast boasts interactive features such as polls, Q&A sessions, and audience engagement analytics. With its focus on fostering community and connection, Cloudcast provides a versatile platform that suits both small-scale gatherings and large-scale virtual conferences.

    25. DaCast

It provides secure content delivery, customizable branding, and monetization options, making it suitable for businesses and individuals alike. With its reliable global content delivery network (CDN) and adaptive bitrate streaming, DaCast ensures a high-quality viewing experience for audiences worldwide.

    26. Uscreen Live Streaming Platform

    Uscreen Live Streaming Platform empowers content creators to build their own branded streaming services. This all-in-one solution offers monetization options, customizable interfaces, and analytics for content performance. Uscreen’s emphasis on providing a branded experience sets it apart, allowing creators to establish their unique identity in the competitive streaming landscape.

    27. Muvi

    Muvi emerges as a comprehensive live streaming platform catering to diverse industries. Offering end-to-end solutions, Muvi empowers users to create, broadcast, and monetize their live events seamlessly. The platform’s white-label capabilities enable customization, allowing businesses to maintain their brand identity. With a focus on scalability and security, Muvi ensures a reliable streaming experience for both broadcasters and viewers. Its versatility makes Muvi an ideal choice for enterprises looking to leverage live streaming for marketing, training, or entertainment purposes.

    28. Brandlive Live Video Streaming

    Brandlive Live Video Streaming specializes in immersive brand experiences. Targeting businesses, the platform enables interactive live events with features like real-time chat, product showcases, and audience engagement tools. Brandlive’s focus on creating memorable brand moments through live video enhances customer connection and brand loyalty, making it a standout choice for companies seeking impactful marketing strategies.

    29. Wowza

    Wowza stands out as a versatile live streaming platform, offering robust solutions for both broadcasting and watching events. With over 280,590 users, Wowza boasts exceptional scalability and reliability. Its advanced features cater to diverse needs, from low-latency streaming to high-quality video delivery. The platform’s flexibility allows seamless integration with various devices and platforms, making it a top choice for content creators and businesses alike.

    30. SproutVideo

    SproutVideo stands out as a versatile live streaming platform for events, combining user-friendly features with top-notch security. Boasting customizable video players and extensive analytics, it caters to diverse streaming needs. SproutVideo ensures a professional look with branding options and supports monetization through paywalls and subscriptions. Its adaptive streaming technology guarantees a smooth viewing experience, making it an ideal choice for businesses looking to deliver high-quality live content.

    31. TargetVideo Live Streaming

    TargetVideo Live Streaming is a user-centric platform that simplifies event broadcasting and consumption. With an emphasis on user experience, it provides easy setup, live chat options, and audience engagement tools. TargetVideo’s adaptive streaming technology ensures seamless playback across devices, reaching a wider audience. The platform prioritizes affordability, making it an attractive option for businesses of all sizes seeking a reliable and cost-effective solution for live streaming events.

    32. StreamingVideoProvider

    StreamingVideoProvider emerges as a comprehensive live streaming platform, offering a feature-rich experience for both broadcasters and viewers. With customizable branding, robust security measures, and adaptive streaming, it caters to diverse event requirements. The platform’s monetization options, including pay-per-view and subscriptions, empower content creators to generate revenue. StreamingVideoProvider’s user-friendly interface, coupled with detailed analytics, enhances the overall streaming experience, making it a go-to choice for businesses and individuals looking to deliver professional and engaging live events.

    33. Funny or Die

    Funny or Die, a unique live streaming platform, takes a humorous approach to event broadcasting. Specializing in comedy content, it provides an engaging space for creators and viewers. Geared towards content creators and businesses, BeLive’s intuitive interface enables easy event broadcasting without the need for technical expertise. BeLive’s emphasis on simplicity, coupled with its powerful features, positions it as a compelling option in the live streaming landscape.

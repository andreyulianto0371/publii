    Top 20 Best Free Movies &amp; TV Shows Streaming Apps (2024) - Quertime

    Andre Yulianto January 19, 2024

    In the era of digital entertainment, streaming apps have become an integral part of our daily lives, offering a plethora of movies and TV shows at our fingertips. The demand for free streaming services has surged, prompting the creation of numerous platforms catering to diverse tastes and preferences. This article explores the top 20 best free movies and TV shows streaming apps, presenting a curated list of applications that deliver quality content without breaking the bank.

    From classic films to trending series, these apps offer a vast library of entertainment suitable for all audiences. Whether you’re a cinephile seeking timeless classics or a binge-watcher in search of the latest shows, this compilation guides you through the world of cost-free streaming, making quality entertainment accessible to everyone. Discover the top free streaming apps that redefine how we experience movies and TV shows, providing an array of options to suit every viewer’s taste.

    1. Netflix – Watch TV Shows and Movies Online (App Store | Google Play)

    Netflix stands as a pioneer in the realm of streaming services, offering an extensive library of movies and TV shows across various genres. With a user-friendly interface and a vast selection of original content, Netflix has become a household name for entertainment enthusiasts. From gripping dramas to light-hearted comedies and groundbreaking documentaries, Netflix caters to diverse tastes, making it a go-to platform for binge-watching.

    2. Amazon Prime Video (App Store | Google Play)

    Amazon Prime Video combines the convenience of shopping with an impressive array of movies and TV shows. As part of the Amazon Prime subscription, users gain access to a diverse collection, including exclusive originals. With a global reach, Prime Video delivers content in multiple languages, ensuring a broad appeal. The platform’s seamless integration with other Amazon services enhances the overall user experience, making it an enticing option for those seeking convenience and quality entertainment.

    3. Disney+ Hotstar (App Store | Google Play)

    Disney+ Hotstar emerges as a powerhouse, marrying the enchanting world of Disney with a wide range of regional and international content. Boasting an extensive catalog of movies, TV series, and sports events, this streaming giant caters to audiences of all ages. From beloved classics to the latest releases, Disney+ Hotstar captivates users with its immersive storytelling and diverse entertainment options.

    4. Hulu – Stream TV Shows and Movies (App Store | Google Play)

    Hulu distinguishes itself by offering a mix of current TV shows, classic favorites, and a robust lineup of original content. Known for its quick turnaround in streaming new episodes shortly after airing, Hulu is a top choice for those who want to stay current with their favorite shows. The platform’s diverse content library and personalized recommendations contribute to its popularity among viewers seeking a dynamic streaming experience.

    5. Peacock TV – Stream TV and Movies (App Store | Google Play)

    Peacock TV, launched by NBCUniversal, positions itself as a one-stop destination for a variety of content, from classic movies to current TV shows and exclusive originals. With a free tier and premium options, Peacock TV caters to a broad audience. Its unique feature, offering live channels and curated thematic collections, adds a layer of dynamism to the streaming experience, making it an enticing choice for those seeking both variety and value.

    6. Just Watch – Movies and TV Shows (App Store | Google Play)

    Navigating the vast ocean of streaming services is no longer a daunting task with JustWatch. This comprehensive platform acts as your personal streaming concierge, helping you find the best movies and TV shows across various free and paid services. JustWatch’s user-friendly interface allows you to search, filter, and discover content effortlessly. Whether you’re in the mood for classic films, trending series, or hidden gems, JustWatch ensures you spend less time scrolling and more time enjoying your favorite entertainment.

    7. Plex – Stream Movies and TV Shows (App Store | Google Play)

    Plex transforms your device into a personalized streaming hub, offering a seamless blend of your local media and online content. This versatile app not only organizes and streams your movie and TV show collection but also provides access to a wide range of free, on-demand content. With Plex, you can curate your media library, enjoy live TV, and explore a world of free entertainment, all within a single, intuitive interface. Elevate your streaming experience with Plex’s user-centric design and unparalleled media management features.

    8. Tubi TV – TV and Filem (App Store | Google Play)

    Tubi TV stands out as a go-to destination for free, ad-supported streaming with a vast library that spans genres and eras. This app offers a diverse selection of movies and TV shows, catering to every taste imaginable. From timeless classics to recent releases, Tubi TV’s user-friendly interface ensures a hassle-free streaming experience. Discover hidden treasures without a subscription fee, and let Tubi TV be your gateway to an expansive world of entertainment.

    9. Roku Channel (App Store | Google Play)

    Roku Channel consolidates an extensive array of free, ad-supported content, making it a one-stop-shop for streaming enthusiasts. This app, designed for Roku users, delivers a seamless streaming experience with a curated selection of movies, TV shows, and exclusive content. Explore a variety of genres, catch up on popular series, and dive into an ever-expanding library of free entertainment. Roku Channel ensures that your streaming journey is not only convenient but also packed with quality content.

    10. YouTube Movies & TV (App Store | Google Play)

    While YouTube is renowned for its user-generated content, it has evolved into a powerhouse for free movies and TV shows. The platform’s official storefront provides a curated selection of films and series that cater to diverse tastes. From blockbuster hits to independent gems, YouTube offers a mix of free and ad-supported content, backed by its familiar and user-friendly interface. Experience the best of both worlds as YouTube seamlessly integrates its extensive video library with a growing collection of free, high-quality entertainment.

    11. Amazon Freevee – Free Movies and TV (App Store | Google Play)

    Amazon Freevee is a robust streaming platform that offers a diverse range of free movies and TV shows. With a user-friendly interface, it provides seamless navigation to a plethora of content without any subscription costs. The platform boasts a curated selection of popular titles across genres, making it an ideal choice for users seeking both classic and contemporary entertainment. From gripping dramas to light-hearted comedies, Amazon Freevee delivers an immersive streaming experience. The inclusion of user-friendly features enhances the overall viewing experience, making it a go-to app for those looking to enjoy high-quality content on their mobile devices.

    12. Crunchyroll (App Store | Google Play)

    Crunchyroll stands as a haven for anime enthusiasts, offering an extensive library of anime series and movies for free streaming. The app is tailored for fans of Japanese animation, providing a diverse collection that spans various genres and caters to a global audience. With subtitles available in multiple languages, Crunchyroll ensures accessibility for a wide range of viewers. The platform’s commitment to delivering the latest episodes shortly after their original airings in Japan adds to its appeal, making it an essential app for anime lovers seeking timely and high-quality content on their mobile devices.

    13. Pluto TV – Watch TV and Movies (App Store | Google Play)

    Pluto TV redefines the streaming experience by offering a vast array of live TV channels and on-demand content without any subscription fees. With a channel lineup covering news, sports, entertainment, and more, Pluto TV provides a cable-like experience on mobile devices. The platform’s intuitive interface and diverse content make it a compelling choice for users looking to explore a variety of channels, including exclusive Pluto TV originals. Whether it’s catching up on current events or enjoying curated movie marathons, Pluto TV stands out as a comprehensive and free streaming solution for mobile users.

    14. Vudu – Buy, Rent and Watch Movies (App Store | Google Play)

    Vudu, owned by Walmart, is a feature-rich streaming app that combines a vast library of free movies with a user-friendly interface. Offering a diverse selection of films, including recent releases and timeless classics, Vudu ensures a cinematic experience on mobile devices. The app’s integration with Movies on Us provides access to an extensive collection of titles without any subscription costs, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious users. Vudu’s high-definition streaming quality and intuitive search functionality make it a convenient and enjoyable platform for movie enthusiasts seeking free and easily accessible content on the go.

    15. Sling – Live TV, Sports and News (App Store | Google Play)

    Sling stands out as a versatile mobile streaming app, offering a mix of live TV channels and on-demand content. While Sling primarily operates as a subscription-based service, it provides a selection of free content that includes news, lifestyle, and entertainment channels. The app’s intuitive interface allows users to navigate effortlessly through the available channels and discover a variety of content. Sling’s commitment to providing a blend of live and on-demand programming makes it a go-to choice for users seeking a customizable and free mobile streaming experience.

    16. hoopla Digital (App Store | Google Play)

    Hoopla Digital is a versatile streaming platform that redefines digital entertainment for library patrons. With a vast and diverse collection of movies, TV shows, music, eBooks, and audiobooks, Hoopla offers a unique experience accessible via your local library. The app seamlessly integrates with library memberships, allowing users to borrow and stream content for free. Hoopla’s user-friendly interface and diverse content make it an excellent choice for those seeking a curated selection of high-quality digital media.

    17. Yidio – Streaming Guide (App Store | Google Play)

    Yidio stands out as a comprehensive streaming aggregator, simplifying the search for your favorite movies and TV shows across various platforms. By consolidating content from popular streaming services like Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime, and more, Yidio enables users to discover, track, and watch their preferred content effortlessly. The app’s intuitive interface and advanced filtering options make it a go-to tool for those who want to streamline their streaming experience and find the best free or subscription-based content available across multiple platforms.

    18. Kanopy – Stream Classic Cinema and Indie Film (App Store | Google Play)

    Kanopy distinguishes itself as an educational and thought-provoking streaming service available for free through participating libraries and universities. Offering an array of documentaries, classic films, and independent cinema, Kanopy appeals to cinephiles and those seeking intellectually stimulating content. Its user-friendly interface, coupled with the ability to access exclusive titles, positions Kanopy as a valuable resource for those who appreciate curated, quality streaming content.

    19. Popcornflix – Movies and TV (App Store | Google Play)

    Popcornflix is a go-to destination for free, ad-supported streaming of movies and TV shows. With a vast library spanning various genres, including drama, comedy, horror, and more, Popcornflix provides an accessible and enjoyable viewing experience. The platform’s straightforward interface and regular content updates make it an ideal choice for users seeking a diverse range of entertainment without the need for a subscription. Popcornflix’s commitment to offering free, quality content makes it a notable player in the world of streaming apps.

    20. Crackle (App Store | Google Play)

    Crackle, Sony’s free streaming service, delivers an extensive library of movies, TV shows, and original content. Boasting a mix of classic favorites and exclusive programming, Crackle offers a compelling entertainment experience without the need for a subscription. The app’s user-friendly design and personalized recommendations cater to a wide audience, making it a popular choice for those who want to explore a variety of content genres without breaking the bank. Crackle’s commitment to providing free and engaging entertainment positions it as a noteworthy contender in the streaming landscape.

    21. Stremio (Google Play)

    Stremio stands out as a versatile and user-friendly streaming app, providing a seamless entertainment experience. Consolidating content from various platforms, Stremio offers a unified interface where users can effortlessly access movies, TV shows, and more. Its sleek design and intuitive features make it a go-to choice for those seeking a centralized hub for streaming content. With a vast library and real-time updates, Stremio ensures users are always connected to their favorite entertainment.

    22. Filmzie – Movie Streaming App (App Store | Google Play)

    Filmzie distinguishes itself in the streaming landscape by emphasizing community-driven curation and a commitment to showcasing independent films. This free mobile app not only offers a diverse range of movies but also empowers users to discover hidden gems. By promoting user-generated recommendations and fostering a sense of discovery, Filmzie fosters a unique streaming environment where independent filmmakers gain visibility, and viewers explore content beyond mainstream offerings.

    23. FilmRise – Movies and TV Shows (App Store | Google Play)

    FilmRise emerges as a prominent free streaming app, providing an extensive collection of movies and TV shows across various genres. With a focus on classic cinema, documentaries, and popular TV series, FilmRise caters to a broad audience. Its commitment to delivering high-quality, free content makes it an appealing option for those seeking a wide range of entertainment without the need for a subscription.

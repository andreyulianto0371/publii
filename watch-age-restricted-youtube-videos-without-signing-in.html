    Watch Age-Restricted YouTube Videos Without Signing In

    Andre Yulianto January 12, 2024

    Let’s say your internet research takes you to a video on YouTube with an age restriction. You want to watch it (you know—for research purposes) but you don’t remember your YouTube login information, don’t want the video in your viewing history. Fortunately, there are a few little URL tricks to help with that.

    If you remove the watch?v= and replace with embed/, you’ll get a link that goes directly to the video without the rest of the YouTube site—and it’ll play full-width in your browser. So, for example, you would take this link:

    youtube.com/watch?v=o5GDrJEGkVk

    and change it to this:

    youtube.com/embed/o5GDrJEGkVk

    And you won’t have to log in. Alternatively, you can add nsfw before the youtube.com portion of the link:

    nsfwyoutube.com/watch?v=o5GDrJEGkVk

    FAQs

    1. Are Scooters suitable for long-distance travel?

    Scooters excel in urban commuting for shorter distances due to their battery limitations. Long-distance travel might require planned recharging stops.

    3. Are Scooters waterproof?

    Scooters can withstand moderate water exposure like light rain or splashes but aren’t entirely waterproof. Avoid heavy rainfall or submersion to prevent potential electrical damage.

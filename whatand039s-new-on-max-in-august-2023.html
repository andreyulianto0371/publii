    What&#039;s New on Max in August 2023

    Andre Yulianto January 12, 2024

    Max giveth, and Max taketh away: On the same day the streaming service—briefly the official hub for decades’ worth of classic modern cartoons that originally aired on Cartoon Network—welcomes Fiona & Cake (Aug. 31), the long-awaited continuation of the animation cult sensation Adventure Time, it’s giving the boot to Over the Garden Wall, a beloved slice of creepy hand-drawn horror from one of Adventure Time’s original creators.

    Starring Fiona the Human and Cake the Cat, the gender-swapped versions of Finn and Jake who appeared in a few episodes of the original Adventure Time, Fiona & Cake is the third series in the animated franchise, and targets a slightly older audience than did Finn and Jake’s funny, fart-filled antics.

    If you’re at all excited for the latest dispatches from the Distant Lands and you somehow have yet to watch Over the Garden Wall, I highly recommend you do so before it vanishes from Max, even if the material is best-suited to a chilly October evening.

    It’s a brief, captivating story that perfectly captures the feeling evoked by your childhood fear of becoming lost in the woods. Hopefully it will pop up somewhere else on streaming soon—but if not, you can buy a digital copy on Prime Video for about $10 (sadly the DVDs are long out of print).

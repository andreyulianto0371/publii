    30 Best Home and Interior Design Online Tools (2024) - Quertime

    Andre Yulianto January 11, 2024

    Designing your home or other empty spaces you have may be simply planned with a paper and pen. But this method wouldn’t give you a good vision of your ideas especially if you’re not a good artist. You may even end up failing in designing your rooms.

    Through the innovation of technology and availability of online tools, preparing and building your home design has become much easier to consume you lesser time and cost. Additionally you may need a construction estimating software to estimate the cost of a construction project. It helps in determining the materials, labor, and other costs involved in a project.

    Without further ado let’s learn about the following tools and enjoy playing with your home ideas and designs.

    1. SketchUp

    SketchUp is a powerful 3D modeling software used for architectural and interior design projects. Its intuitive interface enables users to create detailed models, visualize spaces, and experiment with various designs. With a vast library of pre-made models and plugins, SketchUp allows for intricate detailing and precise measurements. Its versatility caters to professionals and enthusiasts alike, aiding in creating lifelike representations of home interiors.

    2. Houzz

    Houzz is a comprehensive platform merging design inspiration, professional services, and shopping for home-related products. It acts as a hub for homeowners, designers, and contractors, offering a plethora of interior design ideas, curated photos, and access to a network of professionals. Houzz facilitates communication between users and professionals, enabling collaboration, project ideation, and product sourcing within the same platform.

    3. Homebase Room Planners

    Homebase Room Planners provide an interactive way to plan and visualize room layouts and designs. With user-friendly tools and a range of customizable furniture and décor options, users can experiment with different configurations and styles to envision their ideal space. This online tool offered by Homebase helps users accurately plan their room designs and furniture placement before making any purchases.

    4. SmartDraw

    SmartDraw simplifies the design process by offering templates and tools for creating floor plans, diagrams, and flowcharts. While not specifically focused on interior design, it allows users to sketch out room layouts and visualize concepts quickly. SmartDraw’s user-friendly interface and extensive library of shapes and templates make it a versatile tool for creating basic floor plans and visual representations.

    5. Planner 5D

    Planner 5D is an intuitive online tool for creating detailed interior designs and floor plans. It provides a user-friendly interface and a wide range of customizable elements, including furniture, textures, and decor items. With its 2D and 3D modes, Planner 5D enables users to visualize their designs from different perspectives, making it a popular choice for both professionals and homeowners looking to plan and design their spaces effectively.

    6. DreamPlan

    DreamPlan by NCH Software offers comprehensive home and landscape design tools. It allows users to create 3D models of homes, visualize renovations, and design landscapes. With its user-friendly interface and extensive library of objects, textures, and materials, DreamPlan facilitates detailed interior and exterior designs. The software’s intuitive features enable precise measurements and realistic renderings, aiding both professionals and DIY enthusiasts in planning and visualizing their projects.

    7. Floorplanner

    Floorplanner is an intuitive online tool catering to users looking to create detailed floor plans and 3D models. Its drag-and-drop interface allows for easy creation and customization of room layouts, furniture placement, and architectural elements. Floorplanner’s collaborative features enable seamless sharing and editing of designs, making it ideal for professionals working with clients or homeowners planning their spaces.

    8. Coohom

    Coohom is a powerful platform specializing in 3D interior design and visualization. It offers a wide array of customizable furniture, textures, and lighting options, allowing users to create realistic renderings of interior spaces. Coohom’s emphasis on photorealistic visuals, coupled with its user-friendly interface and collaboration features, makes it a preferred choice for interior designers, architects, and homeowners seeking to create and share high-quality designs.

    9. Homestyler

    Homestyler, by Autodesk, provides an intuitive platform for designing interiors in 2D and 3D. With its vast library of furniture, décor items, and real products from various brands, users can experiment with styles and layouts. Its augmented reality feature allows users to visualize designs within their actual space using a mobile device’s camera. Homestyler’s community aspect fosters inspiration and collaboration, making it a go-to tool for homeowners and professionals alike.

    10. Gardena

    Gardena offers a user-friendly online garden planning tool. It allows users to design, plan, and visualize their outdoor spaces, including gardens and landscapes. With features like plant recommendations, automatic irrigation planning, and personalized gardening tips, Gardena assists users in creating and maintaining beautiful outdoor environments. Its intuitive interface and detailed planning capabilities cater to gardening enthusiasts and professionals seeking efficient and visually appealing garden designs.

    11. HomeByMe

    HomeByMe is an intuitive online tool catering to homeowners and professionals alike. It offers a comprehensive platform for creating detailed 3D floor plans and interior designs. With a user-friendly interface, it enables users to visualize spaces, experiment with layouts, and decorate rooms with a vast library of furniture, textures, and decor. Collaboration features allow teams or homeowners and designers to work together seamlessly. The software’s strength lies in its ability to render realistic 3D models, aiding in better decision-making for renovations or new designs. HomeByMe’s accessibility and extensive design options make it an excellent choice for anyone looking to bring their interior visions to life.

    12. Carpetright Visualiser

    Carpetright Visualiser is a specialized tool focused on flooring design. It empowers users to virtually preview how different carpets, vinyl, laminate, and other flooring options would look in their space. Through an easy-to-use interface, individuals can upload pictures of their rooms and experiment with various flooring styles and colors. This tool helps users make informed decisions by visualizing the potential impact of different flooring choices, making the process of selecting the perfect floor covering more engaging and less daunting.

    13. Sweet Home 3D

    Sweet Home 3D is a versatile interior design tool suitable for both professionals and homeowners. It allows users to create 2D floor plans and then visualize them in 3D, making it easier to conceptualize spatial arrangements. The software comes with a vast library of furniture, textures, and objects that users can drag and drop into their designs. Its simplicity and range of features, including the ability to import 3D models, make it an ideal choice for designing rooms, rearranging furniture, or planning renovations.

    14. RoomSketcher

    RoomSketcher is a user-friendly online tool designed to create detailed floor plans and 3D interior designs. It offers a simple drag-and-drop interface for laying out rooms, adding furniture, and experimenting with various design elements. The software also provides features for generating high-quality 3D visualizations, enabling users to envision their spaces realistically. Additionally, RoomSketcher allows easy sharing and collaboration, making it suitable for individuals, real estate professionals, or interior designers seeking efficient ways to create and showcase designs.

    15. Havenly

    Havenly stands out as an online interior design service connecting users with professional designers to curate personalized design solutions. Users begin by taking a style quiz to help match them with a designer whose aesthetic aligns with their preferences. The platform facilitates communication between users and designers, allowing for collaboration on room designs, furniture selections, and decor. Havenly offers various design packages tailored to different budgets and needs, providing an accessible and convenient way to access professional interior design expertise and create beautiful, cohesive spaces.

    16. Graphisoft Archicad

    Graphisoft Archicad is a powerhouse for architects and designers, offering a comprehensive suite of tools for building design and visualization. It’s renowned for its BIM (Building Information Modeling) capabilities, allowing users to create detailed 3D models with precision and efficiency. Archicad’s seamless integration of design and documentation streamlines workflows, enabling real-time collaboration among team members. Its robust visualization features provide stunning renderings and virtual reality experiences, enhancing client presentations and project understanding. With a user-friendly interface and advanced tools for architectural design, Archicad stands out as a top choice for professionals seeking accuracy and innovation in their projects.

    17. Cedreo

    Cedreo is a user-friendly software tailored for home builders and interior designers, facilitating the creation of 3D models and floor plans. Its intuitive interface allows users to craft designs quickly, visualizing them in detailed 3D renderings that can be easily shared with clients. Cedreo’s extensive library of customizable objects and materials enables users to personalize designs to match specific project requirements, simplifying the design process and enhancing client communication. Its focus on simplicity without compromising on functionality makes it a preferred tool for those seeking efficiency in residential design.

    18. Foyr Neo

    Foyr Neo is an all-in-one interior design software that caters to designers, decorators, and architects alike. With its emphasis on user-friendliness and powerful design capabilities, Neo offers an array of features to create stunning visualizations. Its drag-and-drop interface, coupled with an extensive catalog of furniture and decor items, allows for swift creation and customization of spaces. Neo’s real-time rendering engine provides lifelike visuals, enabling users to present designs convincingly. Its collaborative features foster seamless communication among team members and clients, making it an ideal choice for professionals aiming for both efficiency and visual impact in their projects.

    19. Decorilla

    Decorilla is an online interior design platform that connects users with professional interior designers to transform their spaces virtually. It offers personalized design services through a streamlined process that involves client input, designer collaboration, and tailored design concepts. Decorilla’s platform allows clients to visualize and experience their future space through mood boards, 3D renderings, and shopping lists curated by experienced designers. Its emphasis on client-designer interaction and convenience makes it an excellent choice for those seeking expert guidance and customized interior design solutions.

    20. Chief Architect

    Chief Architect is a robust software suite catering to architects, builders, and remodelers, offering sophisticated tools for 2D and 3D architectural design. Its comprehensive feature set allows users to create detailed floor plans, elevations, and cross-sections with precision. Chief Architect’s library of architectural objects and materials provides a vast array of options for creating realistic renderings and walkthroughs. The software’s Building Information Modeling (BIM) capabilities streamline the design process, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. Its professional-grade tools and emphasis on detailed documentation make it a go-to solution for those in need of comprehensive architectural design and visualization.

    21. Magicplan

    Magicplan revolutionizes space planning with its user-friendly app. Using augmented reality and precise measurement tools, it enables users to create floor plans effortlessly. By simply scanning a room with a smartphone, it generates accurate dimensions and allows customization with various design elements. It’s perfect for DIY enthusiasts and professionals alike, offering seamless integration into design workflows.

    22. RoomStyler 3D Home Planner

    This online tool elevates interior design by offering a user-friendly interface and powerful features. With RoomStyler, users can visualize their design ideas in 3D, experiment with furniture arrangements, and explore various styles. Its extensive catalog of furniture and decor items enables realistic room simulations, fostering creativity and aiding decision-making for design projects.

    23. Roomtodo – Room Planner

    Roomtodo simplifies room planning with its intuitive interface. It allows users to create detailed floor plans, experiment with furniture layouts, and visualize designs in 3D. Its collaboration features enable seamless teamwork, making it a go-to choice for both professionals and homeowners.

    24. Autodesk Revit

    Autodesk Revit is a powerful software tailored for professionals in architecture and building design. It offers comprehensive tools for creating detailed 3D models, generating construction documentation, and facilitating collaboration among project stakeholders. Its Building Information Modeling (BIM) capabilities streamline the design process, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in creating complex architectural designs.

    25. Dulux Visualizer App

    The Dulux Visualizer app brings paint selection to life. By using augmented reality, it allows users to see how different paint colors would look on their walls in real-time. With an extensive color palette and the ability to save and share designs, it simplifies the often daunting task of choosing the right paint color for any room, empowering users to confidently make color decisions for their spaces.

    26. Plan Your Room

    Plan Your Room is an intuitive tool allowing users to design and visualize rooms effortlessly. With its user-friendly interface, it enables individuals to create floor plans, experiment with furniture placement, and explore various design elements. Offering a wide range of furniture items and decor options, it allows for detailed customization. Its drag-and-drop functionality simplifies the process, making it suitable for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts. Plan Your Room provides a 3D view of designs, aiding users in visualizing their spaces realistically before implementation.

    27. Autodesk AutoCAD LT

    Autodesk AutoCAD LT is a powerful software tailored for professionals in the design and engineering industry. Renowned for its precision and robust drafting tools, AutoCAD LT enables users to create precise 2D drawings and documentation. With its comprehensive suite of features, it streamlines workflows, allowing for efficient collaboration and accurate designs. Its extensive libraries and customizable options cater to various design needs, making it a staple in architectural and engineering firms.

    28. Spoak

    Spoak is an online platform that facilitates seamless collaboration between homeowners and interior designers. It connects users with a curated network of designers, simplifying the process of finding the perfect match for their project. Spoak offers personalized design recommendations based on user preferences, ensuring a tailored experience. The platform streamlines communication and project management, fostering a smooth and efficient design journey for clients and designers alike.

    29. Mydoma Studio

    Mydoma Studio is a comprehensive interior design software designed to streamline the design process. It offers tools for project management, client communication, invoicing, and design boards. Mydoma Studio centralizes project information, allowing designers to efficiently manage tasks and collaborate with clients. With its intuitive interface and customizable features, it enhances workflow efficiency and client satisfaction, making it a valuable tool for interior design professionals.

    30. Live Home 3D

    Live Home 3D is a user-friendly home design app suitable for both professionals and enthusiasts. With its easy-to-use interface and extensive library of objects, furniture, and textures, Live Home 3D simplifies the process of creating 2D floor plans and 3D renderings. The software allows users to visualize their designs in real-time, offering precise measurements and accurate representations of spaces. Its cross-platform compatibility and integration with VR technologies enhance the design experience, making it a versatile tool for home design and renovation projects.

    Conclusion

    Having to design may be perceived as a hassle or something fun depending on the interest of a person. But with these handy programs that are very easy and helpful to use, designing has become open and free for everyone to try and even know design mistakes. Because of this, you now don’t have to spend a lot in hiring an interior designer or buying wrong choices of products, but become a designer yourself from these amazing online tools.

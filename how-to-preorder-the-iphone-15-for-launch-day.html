    How to Preorder the iPhone 15 for Launch Day

    Andre Yulianto January 12, 2024

    Now that Apple’s Wonderlust event is officially over, all four models of the iPhone 15 series will soon be able to preorder. If you’re interested in picking up an iPhone 15 or 15 Plus, or splurging for one of the 15 Pros, here’s how to make sure you order yours as quickly as possible.

    What’s new with the iPhone 15 and 15 Pro?

    The iPhone 15 lineup is similar to last year: The 6.1-inch iPhone 15 and the 6.7 inch iPhone 15 Plus features a two camera setup, with the main camera now updated to a 48MP sensor. This year, they both get the Dynamic Island from the iPhone 14 Pro, as well as the 14 Pro’s A16 Bionic chip, which will provide a processing boost over the A15 found in the iPhone 14. Notably, all new iPhones charge via USB-C, replacing the Lightning port found on every iPhone since 2012.

    The iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max include all the new features from the 15, and then some. They have a lighter Titanium body design, and a smaller body footprint as well. Plus, the three-camera setup has been updated with larger sensors. The Pro Max gets a huge 5X optical zoom lens, exclusive to the most premium phone. The mute switch makes way for the Action button, opening up a world of shortcuts. And the Pro iPhones support the faster USB 3 standard, allowing for up to 10 Gbps transfer speeds.

    How to preorder the new iPhone 15 or 15 Pro

    All four iPhone 15 devices will be available to preorder at 8 a.m. ET on Friday, Sept. 15, and they will ship a week later, on Sept. 22nd. Apple’s online store is live with all the configurations, prices, and color options, so you can take your time to decide which version you want, but you can’t place the order just yet. For that, you’ll need to wait until Friday morning.

    The iPhone 15 starts at $799 for 128 GB storage, while the iPhone 15 Plus starts at $899. You can add $100 to bump up the storage to 256GB, or 512GB for $300 extra.

    The iPhone 15 Pro, on the other hand, starts at $999, and can be maxed out with 1TB storage space for $1,499. The iPhone 15 Pro Max now starts at $1,199 for 256GB of storage. The maxed out 1TB model will set you back $1,499 for the 15 Pro, and $1,599 for the 15 Pro Max.

    Ready to preorder? Use these links to reserve your iPhone(s):

    • iPhone 15 starting at $799

    • iPhone 15 Plus starting at $899

    • iPhone 15 Pro starting at $999

    • iPhone 15 Pro Max starting at $1,199

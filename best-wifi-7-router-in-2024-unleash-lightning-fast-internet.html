    FAQs

    How Fast Is WiFi 7?

    The WiFi 7 technology is five times faster compared to WiFi 6E and it increases the maximum speeds from 9.6 Gbps to 46 Gbps.

    This allows you to enjoy the current ultra-fast internet plans with better performance and less interference when you are involved in high-bandwidth online activities.

    Are WiFi 7 Routers Backwards Compatible?

    WiFi 7 standard is backward compatible like all the previous WiFi generations. This means that you can connect your current devices to a WiFi 7 broadcaster as well as connect a router of an older standard to a WiFi 7 client.

    However, to take full advantage of WiFi 7, you need to buy new hardware on both ends of the connection.

    Is Getting A WiFi 7 Router Worth It?

    Getting a WiFi 7 router depends on your existing equipment. If you have an older router that supports WiFi 5 standard, then upgrading to a WiFi 7 router is worth it as you will experience a significant boost in performance.

    But, if your router is new and supports WiFi 6E standard, you can still use it for a bit before upgrading.

    Does WiFi 7 Improve WiFi Range And Coverage?

    With lower interference and higher speeds, you will experience an overall improvement in coverage, especially as applications that need higher speeds become available.

    With technological advancements like multi-link operation, you will notice an improvement in your WiFi’s seamless roaming and performance across coverage areas of any size and mesh networks.
